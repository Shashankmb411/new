Abstract

This research portal provides access to innovative research papers related to smart helmet technology, IoT-based safety systems, accident detection, GPS tracking, and intelligent transportation safety solutions. The purpose of this platform is to help students, researchers, and technology enthusiasts explore advanced embedded systems and IoT-based safety applications that improve rider protection and road safety. By integrating smart sensors, communication modules, and real-time monitoring systems, these technologies aim to reduce accidents and enhance emergency response mechanisms.
